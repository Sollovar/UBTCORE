================================================================================
WHAT WAS THE PROBLEM?
================================================================================

Price-worker was updating shared database columns (price, price_usd) every 39 
seconds with GeckoTerminal data, overwriting your backend's exchange prices 
(computed from actual fills/orderbook).

Result: UI showed gecko prices everywhere, even in places meant to show 
actual exchange prices.

================================================================================
THE SOLUTION
================================================================================

Professional database-level separation:
  - Created dedicated gecko_* columns for GeckoTerminal market prices
  - Kept original columns for exchange prices (from your DEX fills)
  - Updated price-worker to write ONLY to gecko_* columns
  - Backend computes exchange prices from fills (price columns)
  - Frontend displays both prices in appropriate places

Result: Complete independence - gecko and exchange prices never conflict!
